## Per-app lockfile cleanup

`create-next-app` and `create-rsbuild` each write a `package-lock.json` inside the app directory they scaffold. In a workspaces setup those lockfiles are never consulted again — the root lockfile is authoritative — so they freeze on day one and show up in Dependabot scans reporting pre-workspace-hoist vulnerabilities. Both scaffold flows now `rm` the per-app lockfile right after the upstream tool finishes.
