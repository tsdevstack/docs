# v0.2.6

Released: April 14, 2026

---

- **CLI hints rewritten**: `init`, `generate-docker-compose`, and `generate-kong` next-steps no longer recommend raw `docker compose` commands. They now point users at `npx tsdevstack sync` (which is the supported orchestration path), and `cloud:init` is shown as the last step since it isn't needed for local development.
- **OWASP compliance docs**: auth template and infrastructure already followed OWASP guidelines; this release documents that. Adds OWASP Top 10 coverage table, cheat-sheet alignment notes, and references throughout the user-facing docs and READMEs.
